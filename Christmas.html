Christmas Tree Lightning

We are preparing and cutting materials for a recyclable project to craft a white sheep. Using recycled plastic bottles, we carefully shape and assemble the parts. Teamwork and innovation are the heart of this project.

Gathered under the radiant Christmas tree, this group celebrates the holiday season during a school Christmas lighting event. The warm lights and vibrant decorations create the perfect backdrop for a memorable evening. It's a moment of togetherness, joy, and holiday cheer!
