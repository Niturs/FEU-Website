FEU Roosevelt Halloween

The Halloween party at FEU was full of excitement, with students dressed in fun costumes. Kids went around collecting treats and enjoying the festive atmosphere. Everyone had a great time celebrating the spooky season together. The event was a great way to foster a sense of community and creativity, making it a memorable occasion for all involved.

The FEU Halloween Cosplay event brought vibrant energy to the campus as students from various levels showcased their creativity by dressing up as characters from different genres. The atmosphere was lively, with students passionately portraying their chosen personas through detailed costumes and performances. The event featured a mix of outdoor activities, stage presentations, and indoor gatherings, where participants celebrated their enthusiasm for cosplay and Halloween festivities, creating a memorable and colorful celebration.
