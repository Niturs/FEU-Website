Sinag Sports

Sinag Sports at FEU Roosevelt is not just a sports event it’s a time to show skills, bond, and celebrate school pride! Named after "sinag," which means rays of light in Filipino, it stands as a symbol of teamwork and joy. Come and watch students, teachers, and staff as they play in sports like basketball, volleyball, and more! Whether you’re playing to win, cheering for your team, or making cool banners, there’s something for everyone in this lively sports fest. With thrilling games, fun rivalries, and special memories, Sinag Sports shows what hard work, teamwork, and pushing limits look like the best of what FEU Roosevelt stands for. Let’s come together, play, and shine bright with Sinag Sports!
